Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 21st September 2025
Chore No More / Corunna Developers

Chore No More ("the App") is developed and operated by Corunna Developers ("we," "our," or "us"). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use Chore No More.

If you have any questions, please contact us at admin@chorenomore.app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use Chore No More:

  • Account Information: Your email address (required for account creation and login). You may also choose to set a display name.
  • Family & Chore Data: Information you provide such as family members, chores, schedules, and related activity.
  • Timezone Data: Automatically collected to ensure chores, schedules, and timestamps work correctly in your local region.
  • Subscription Data: Information related to your premium subscription status, managed exclusively through the Apple App Store. We do not collect or store your payment details.
  • Analytics & Crash Reports: Anonymous information about app usage and performance to help us improve the service.
  • Authentication Data: We integrate with Supabase, Apple, and Google authentication services. These providers may collect certain information as part of their login process.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Chore No More service.
  • Enable core features such as family management, chore tracking, and scheduling.
  • Manage and validate subscriptions via the Apple App Store.
  • Improve the app experience through analytics and crash reporting.
  • Securely authenticate and authorize access to your account.

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

3. Sharing of Information

We may share limited data with trusted third-party providers who help us operate the app:

  • Supabase (database and backend services).
  • Apple and Google (authentication and subscriptions).
  • Analytics and crash reporting providers (for performance and reliability).

These providers only have access to the information necessary to perform their services and are obligated to protect it.

4. Children's Privacy

Chore No More is designed for family use, which may include children under the age of 13.

  • Parents or guardians must create and oversee accounts for children.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 without parental consent.
  • If we discover that a child has provided personal information without consent, we will delete it.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us information without consent, please contact us at admin@chorenomore.app.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

  • You can delete your account and associated data at any time using the in-app settings.
  • We will retain your information only as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by law.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of stored and transmitted data via Supabase and secure Apple/Google authentication.
  • Regular monitoring and updates to maintain security.

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your data, such as the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You may exercise these rights by contacting us at admin@chorenomore.app or using the in-app settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Effective Date" above and may notify you within the app.
